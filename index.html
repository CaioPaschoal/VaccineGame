Vaccine, GO!

Game "Vaccine, GO!"

The game Vaccine, GO! is based on the 90's classic Snake Game mobile, and it aims to remind you about the importance of vaccines against viruses.

Instructions
  • Move your character
  • Avoid the virus!
  • Get the syringes!
  • Increase your group
Refresh
Controls
  • or W: UP
  • or A: DOWN
  • or S: LEFT
  • or D: RIGHT

Score: 0

Game developed by Caio Paschoal, inspired by the project of Gabriela Pinheiro for the Digital Innovation One
